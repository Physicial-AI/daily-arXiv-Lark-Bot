### [1] [Flat-Pack Bench: Evaluating Spatio-Temporal Understanding in Large Vision-Language Models through Furniture Assembly](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.21625)
*Aditya Chetan,Eric Cai,Peeyush Kushwaha,Bharath Raj Nagoor Kani,Utkarsh Mall,Qianqian Wang,Noah Snavely,Bharath Hariharan*

Main category: cs.CV

TL;DR: 提出Flat-Pack Bench基准，用于评估大视觉语言模型在细粒度时空推理任务上的表现，特别是家具组装场景中的动作排序、状态定位、部件配合等能力。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 现有视频理解基准主要关注粗粒度任务（如动作分割、分类、描述），且依赖容易用语言描述的实体（如家居物品、动物、人物），无法有效评估模型在复杂真实场景中的细粒度时空理解能力，而许多应用（如家具组装、烹饪）需要这种能力。

Method: 提出Flat-Pack Bench基准，专注于家具组装任务，通过多项选择题配合视觉提示（高亮相关部件）来评估LVLMs在四个细粒度任务上的表现：组装动作的时间排序、组装状态的时间定位、部件配合理解、以及跟踪能力。

Result: 实验表明，当前最先进的大视觉语言模型在细粒度时空推理任务上表现显著不足，特别是在有效利用视频时间信息、跟踪能力、以及理解空间交互（如物理接触）方面存在明显局限。

Conclusion: Flat-Pack Bench基准填补了现有视频理解基准在细粒度时空推理评估方面的空白，揭示了当前LVLMs在复杂真实场景理解上的局限性，为该领域的研究提供了新的评估方向。

Abstract: The emergence of Large Vision-Language Models (LVLMs) has significantly advanced video understanding capabilities. However, existing benchmarks focus predominantly on coarse-grained tasks such as action segmentation, classification, captioning, and retrieval. Furthermore, these benchmarks often rely on entities that can be easily identified verbally, like household objects, animals, human subjects, etc., limiting their applicability to complex, in-the-wild video scenarios. But, many applications such as furniture assembly, cooking, etc., require step-by-step fine-grained spatio-temporal understanding of the video, which is not sufficiently evaluated in current benchmarks. To address this gap, we introduce Flat-Pack Bench, a novel benchmark centered on furniture assembly tasks. Our benchmark evaluates LVLMs on nuanced tasks, including temporal ordering of assembly actions, temporal localization of assembly state, understanding part mating, and tracking, using multiple-choice questions paired with visual prompts highlighting relevant parts as references for fine-grained questions. Our experiments reveal that state-of-the-art LVLMs struggle significantly with fine-grained spatio-temporal reasoning, highlighting their limitations in effectively leveraging temporal information from videos, limited tracking ability, and understanding of spatial interactions like physical contact.

### [2] [Bounding-Box Trajectories Matter for Video Anomaly Detection](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.21957)
*Inpyo Song,Jangwon Lee*

Main category: cs.CV

TL;DR: TrajVAD 是一个利用边界框轨迹作为主要异常检测线索的视频异常检测框架，通过归一化流学习正常运动模式，在仅使用轨迹信息时就能超越基于姿态的方法。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 视频异常检测对公共安全至关重要，但现有方法面临挑战。基于人体姿态的方法虽然表现良好，但往往忽略了边界框轨迹信息，而这些信息在姿态检测流程中本就可用，是一个未被充分探索的有效模态。

Method: 提出 TrajVAD 框架，使用归一化流对多类别边界框轨迹进行建模，学习正常的运动学模式。包含两个变体：仅使用轨迹的 TrajVAD-T 和结合姿态信息的 TrajVAD-P。

Result: TrajVAD-T 在 ShanghaiTech 数据集上达到 87.7% AP，超越所有基于姿态的方法。TrajVAD-P 进一步提升至 88.6% AUROC 和 90.9% AP，在 MSAD 数据集上也取得最佳结果。

Conclusion: 边界框轨迹是一种有效但未被充分探索的视频异常检测模态，TrajVAD 框架通过利用这一信息显著提升了检测性能，证明了轨迹信息的重要价值。

Abstract: Video anomaly detection is critical for public safety and security, yet remains highly challenging despite extensive research due to large variations in appearance, viewpoint, and scene dynamics. Among existing approaches, human pose-based methods have emerged as a major line of research, showing strong performance since many anomalies in public datasets involve humans and pose representations are robust to appearance changes while providing compact motion descriptions. However, these methods often overlook bounding-box trajectories, although such information is inherently available in pose-based pipelines. In this paper, we explicitly leverage these trajectories as a primary anomaly cue. We present TrajVAD, a framework that models multi-class bounding-box trajectories using normalizing flows to learn normal kinematic patterns. Its trajectory-only variant (TrajVAD-T) eliminates pose estimation and surpasses all compared pose-based methods on ShanghaiTech in AP (87.7%), while achieving the best results on MSAD. An extended version (TrajVAD-P) incorporates pose information and further improves performance to 88.6% AUROC and 90.9% AP on ShanghaiTech, highlighting bounding-box trajectories as an effective yet underexplored modality for video anomaly detection.

### [3] [Alike Parts: A Feature-Informed Approach to Local and Global Prototype Explanations](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.21646)
*Jacek Karolczak,Jerzy Stefanowski*

Main category: cs.LG

TL;DR: 该论文提出了一个结合特征重要性的原型解释框架，通过"相似部分"方法在局部解释中突出实例与原型之间的共享特征子集，并在全局原型选择中引入特征多样性目标，实验表明该方法能保持或提高预测保真度。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 原型解释虽然直观，但缺乏特征层面的粒度。现有方法通常只关注实例与原型的整体相似性，而忽略了哪些具体特征对解释最为重要。需要一种能同时提供实例级和特征级解释的原型框架。

Method: 提出一个两层次框架：1) 局部解释：使用"相似部分"方法，通过特征重要性分数突出分类实例与其最近原型之间最相关的共享特征子集；2) 全局原型选择：在原型选择目标函数中加入特征重要性项，主动促进所选原型的特征多样性。

Result: 在六个基准数据集上的实验表明，增强的原型选择过程保持或提高了替代模型的预测保真度，说明特征多样性不会损害模型保真度。

Conclusion: 该框架成功地将特征重要性整合到原型解释中，既提供了细粒度的特征级解释，又通过促进特征多样性保持了模型性能，增强了原型解释的实用性和可解释性。

Abstract: Prototype-based explanations offer an intuitive, example-based approach to support the interpretability of machine learning black box classifiers but often lack feature-level granularity. We introduce a framework that integrates feature importance at two levels to address this gap. First, for local explanations, we propose \textit{alike parts}: a method that uses feature importance scores to highlight the most relevant, shared feature subsets between a classified instance and its nearest prototype, guiding user attention. Second, we augment the global prototype selection objective function with a feature importance term to actively promote diversity in the feature attributions of the selected prototypes. Experiments on six benchmark datasets show that this augmented selection process maintains or, in some cases, increases the prediction fidelity of the surrogate model, suggesting that feature diversity does not compromise model fidelity.

### [4] [Non-Contact Vibration-Based Damage Detection of Civil Structures Using a Cost-Effective Autonomous UAV](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.21914)
*Javier Becerril,Maximiliano Vargas,Jennifer Herrera,Joanna Gutierrez,Jorge Rios,Mohsen Amjadian,Constantine Tarawneh,Jinghao Yang,Qi Lu*

Main category: cs.RO

TL;DR: 本文提出了一种基于非接触式振动的结构损伤检测方法，使用自主定制的低成本无人机，通过视频提取振动信号识别结构损伤引起的频率变化。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 传统接触式结构健康监测在难以接触或GPS受限环境中不实用，需要开发低成本、自主的非接触式检测方案。

Method: 使用定制低成本无人机搭载摄像头，通过视觉运动跟踪从视频中提取振动信号，在频域分析位移时间序列，识别自然频率变化。

Result: 所有平台（智能手机、USB相机、无人机）均成功捕捉到基频及其损伤引起的偏移，无人机误差略高（最高5.7%）但能可靠检测损伤引起的频率变化。

Conclusion: 低成本自主无人机为结构健康监测提供了实用、灵活、可扩展的解决方案，特别适用于接触式传感不切实际的场景，并支持部署多无人机协同增强检测覆盖和鲁棒性。

Abstract: This paper presents a non-contact approach for vibration-based structural damage detection using an autonomous and customized cost-effective unmanned aerial vehicle (UAV). Vibration signals are extracted from video recordings through vision-based motion tracking to identify shifts in natural frequencies indicative of structural degradation. A laboratory-scale frame structure is evaluated under healthy and simulated-damage conditions. The proposed system is validated through an experimental study involving two smartphones, a USB camera, and a custom-built low-cost UAV equipped with an onboard camera and an autonomous alignment system for operation in GPS-denied environments. The displacement time is extracted and analyzed in the frequency domain and compared to reference measurements from contact accelerometers and a finite element model. Experimental results show that all platforms successfully capture the fundamental frequency and its shift due to damage. Although the UAV exhibits slightly higher errors (up to 5.7%) due to platform-induced disturbances and sensing limitations, it reliably detects damage-induced frequency changes. Compared to commercial UAV systems, the proposed platform achieves comparable inspection performance at significantly lower cost. These results demonstrate that low-cost autonomous UAVs provide a practical, flexible, and scalable solution for structural health monitoring, particularly in scenarios where contact-based sensing is impractical. The findings also support the potential for the deployment of multiple cooperative UAVs to further enhance inspection coverage and robustness.

### [5] [Learning to Evolve: Multi-modal Interactive Fields for Robust Humanoid Navigation in Dynamic Environments](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.21935)
*Peifeng Jiang,Hong Liu,Jin Jin,Wenshuai Wang,Xia Li*

Main category: cs.RO

TL;DR: MIF是一个面向人形机器人的多模态交互场系统，通过结合置信感知的语义3D高斯泼溅、差异触发的空间记忆更新和任务驱动的几何重建，在动态环境中实现可靠的操作导向导航。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 人形机器人在操作导向导航中需要可靠的场景记忆，但现有语义建图和场景图系统难以应对运动引起的感知失真、环境变化和交互级几何安全约束，因为它们通常假设稳定的相机轨迹、静态环境或粗略的物体几何。

Method: MIF系统包含三个耦合的场：1)不确定性感知的3DGS外观场，抑制步态引起的模糊；2)空间场，维护拓扑记忆；3)几何场，支持操作前的交互姿态安全。引入差异检测评分来区分运动引起的假阳性变化和持久变化，仅更新局部不一致区域。

Result: 在Unitree-G1人形机器人的真实动态办公室环境中，MIF将非静态环境中的重定位成功率从12%提升到94%（相比静态场景图记忆），同时通过特征蒸馏将语义记忆占用减少91.4%，实现实用的在线操作。

Conclusion: MIF系统通过多场耦合和差异触发的记忆更新机制，为人形机器人在动态环境中提供了可靠的操作导向导航能力，显著提升了重定位性能并大幅减少了内存占用。

Abstract: Safe manipulation-oriented navigation for humanoid robots requires scene memory that remains reliable under locomotion-induced perceptual distortion, environmental changes, and interaction-level geometric safety constraints. Existing semantic mapping and scene-graph systems are difficult to deploy directly in this setting because they often assume stable camera trajectories, static environments, or coarse object geometry. We introduce the Multi-modal Interactive Field (MIF), a humanoid-oriented system that integrates confidence-aware semantic 3D Gaussian Splatting, discrepancy-triggered spatial memory updates, and task-driven geometric reconstruction within a closed-loop perception-adaptation pipeline. MIF couples three fields: an uncertainty-aware 3DGS Appearance Field that suppresses gait-induced blur, a Spatial Field that maintains topological memory, and a Geometry Field that supports Interaction Pose Safety (IPS) before manipulation. A discrepancy detection score is introduced to separate locomotion-induced false-positive changes from persistent changes and updates only locally inconsistent regions. On a Unitree-G1 humanoid in a real dynamic office, MIF improves relocation success in non-static environments from 12% to 94% compared with static scene-graph memory, while reducing semantic memory footprint by 91.4% through feature distillation for practical online operation.
